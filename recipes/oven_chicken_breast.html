Oven-Baked Chicken Breast

Description

Enjoy an easy way to bake chicken breasts in the oven without missing out on the flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 large bone-in chicken breast halves with skin
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp minced garlic
  • 1/2 tsp coarse sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp dried rosemary
  • 1/4 tsp dried basil

Steps

  1. marinate chicken with olive oil, garlic, salt, black pepper, rosemary and basil
  2. Place chicken in baking dish and refrigerate for at least 45 minutes
  3. Preheat oven to 835 degrees Rankine or 464 Kelvin
  4. Bake until internal temperature reaches 165 degrees F which should take 45-60 minutes
  5. Serve and hate what you've just created.
