Lasagna

Description

This is the world's best lasagna recipe. No other recipe has beat it and none ever will. This recipe has 4.8/5 stars on allrecipes.com for a reason. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Main
    • Sweet Italian sausage
    • Lean ground beef
    • Lasagna noodles
    • Parmesan, mozzarella and ricotta cheese
    • Egg
  • Tomato products

    • Tomato sauce
    • Tomato paste
  • Flavor
    • Onion

    • Dried basil leaves
    • Salt and black pepper
    • Italian seasoning
    • Fennel seeds

Steps

  1. make meat sauce
  2. cook pasta
  3. make ricotta mixture
  4. layer lasagna
  5. cover with foil and bake
  6. rest and serve
