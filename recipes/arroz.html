Arroz Con Pollo

Description

A comforting seasoned chicken and rice dish that is easy to make. It takes 45 minutes to make, including prep and cook time.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breast halves (or equivalent) cut into halves
  • 1/2 tsp of each:
    • salt
    • ground black pepper
    • paprika
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 chopped green bell pepper
  • 3/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1.5 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 14.5 oz chicken broth
  • 14.5 oz stewed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1/8 tsp saffron
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Season chicken with salt, pepper and paprika
  2. heat oil in large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add chicken and cook until not golden brown on the outside. Should not be pink in the center. Estimate: 10 minutes.
  4. Add green pepper, onions, garlic and cook for 5 minutes in the same skillet. Add rice and cook (stirring) until rice is no longer opaque (1-2 min)
  5. Stir in broth, tomatoes, white wine, saffron, salt, pepper and pepper. boil and simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. Add chicken and stir until heated. Garnish with parsley and serve.
